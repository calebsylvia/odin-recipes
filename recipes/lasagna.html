Lasagna

The World's Best Lasagna recipe made nice and easy for you even a dog can make it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices/Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil to bake
  6. Let rest before serving
